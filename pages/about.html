Who We Are

Technology That Creates Real Value

Founded in 2013 in Pune, Teivora emerged from a single observation: most technology vendors sold tools, not outcomes. We set out to be different.

Powered by 3rd Eye Imagination — our philosophy of seeing beyond the obvious — Teivora has grown from a 3-person startup to a 40+ team serving clients across 15 countries. We remain obsessively focused on outcomes: not what we build, but what our clients achieve because of it.
